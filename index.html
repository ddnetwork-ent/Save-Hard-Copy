About DD Network

Learn more about our company and what drives us

Founded in 2025 by Danial Danish, DD Network is a small but passionate web services team dedicated to helping individuals and businesses get online easily and affordably.

We specialize in creating simple HTML websites and providing domains for those who need them, while also offering monitoring and basic support to make sure your website stays online and running smoothly.

At DD Network, we believe that everyone should be able to have a professional online presence, whether it's a personal portfolio, a small business site, or a growing company's first website. Our goal is to make the process easy, affordable, and stress-free for our clients.
